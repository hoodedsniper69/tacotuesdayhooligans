What We’re About

We’re a laid-back Northern Michigan moto crew hosting open Tuesday meet-ups and scenic rides. Our focus is bringing all bikers together — no matter what they ride — to break down barriers and build community. We meet at Classic Motorsports of TC at 6:00 PM, kickstands up at 6:30 PM, and ride to a local Northern Michigan diner to support local businesses. Along the way, we give back through charity runs and merch sales that help local causes.

Affiliations & friends: ABATE of Michigan (Region 22), Classic Motorsports – Traverse City, and Ride With Purpose.
